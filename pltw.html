Project Lead The Way (PLTW)

Our Computer Science program follows the Project Lead The Way curriculum, providing students with a comprehensive foundation in computer science principles and practices.

PLTW Courses We Offer:

  • Computer Science Essentials
  • AP Computer Science Principles
  • AP Computer Science A
  • Cybersecurity

Through these courses, students develop problem-solving skills, learn programming fundamentals, and gain exposure to various aspects of computer science.
